Frequently Asked Questions

Who runs the newsletter?

The newsletter is produced and edited by Misty Daniels, the Fort Peck Tribes Media Coordinator and Chairmans Secretary.

How often is the newsletter released?

The newsletter is released bi-weekly on wednesdays.

Where can I view the newsletter?

The newsletter can be found on this webpage under the "Latest Issue" tab, or on our Facebook page.

Where can I get a physical copy of the newsletter?

We are currently working on making physical copies of the FPT Newsletter, however we are still in the early stages of development so securing production, distribution, and funding for print are still being navigated.
