Frequently Asked Questions

Who runs the newsletter?

The FPT Newsletter is produced and edited by Misty Daniels, the Fort Peck Tribes Media Coordinator and Chairmans Secretary.

How often is the newsletter released?

The FPT Newsletter is released Wednesdays, bi-weekly.

Where can I view the newsletter?

The FPT Newsletter can be found on this webpage under the "Latest Issue" tab, or on our Facebook page.

Where can I get a physical copy of the newsletter?

We are currently working on making physical copies of the FPT Newsletter, however we are still in the early stages of development so securing production, distribution, and funding for print are still being navigated.

Why was the newsletter created?

The FPT Newsletter was created as a source of current and reliable information from the Tribes to the public.
